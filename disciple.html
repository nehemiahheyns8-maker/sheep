Disciples of Christ

What is a Disciple?

A disciple is a follower of Jesus Christ — not just by name, but by lifestyle. Jesus said: “If anyone would come after me, let him deny himself and take up his cross daily and follow me.” (Luke 9:23). A disciple learns from Christ and lives like Christ, showing His love to others.

The Purpose of a Disciple

• To love God with all their heart, soul, and mind (Matthew 22:37).
• To love others as Christ has loved us (John 13:34).
• To go into the world and make disciples of all nations (Matthew 28:19–20).
• To shine as a light in a dark world (Matthew 5:14–16).

Encouragement

Being a disciple has nothing to do with age, status, or wealth. Whether young or old, weak or strong, every believer is called to follow Jesus and bring others to Him. You can start by showing kindness, sharing the Gospel, or simply living in a way that reflects Christ.
