Kids’ Corner 🌟

Fun and simple Bible stories for children

🐳 Jonah and the Big Fish

Jonah didn’t listen to God and tried to run away. But God sent a big fish to swallow him. Jonah prayed, and God forgave him. He then preached God’s message, and people repented!

Lesson: God gives second chances when we turn back to Him.

🦁 Daniel in the Lions’ Den

Daniel prayed to God even when it was forbidden. The king’s men threw him into the lions’ den. But God sent an angel to shut the lions’ mouths, and Daniel was safe!

Lesson: Trust God no matter what—He protects His children.

⛵ Jesus Calms the Storm

One day, Jesus and His disciples were on a boat. A big storm scared everyone, but Jesus said, “Peace, be still!” and the storm stopped.

Lesson: Jesus brings peace when we are afraid.
