Kids’ Corner 🌟

Fun and simple Bible stories for children

🐳 Jonah and the Big Fish

Jonah didn’t listen to God and tried to run away. But God sent a big fish to swallow him. Jonah prayed, and God forgave him. He then preached God’s message, and people repented!

Lesson: God gives second chances when we turn back to Him.

🦁 Daniel in the Lions’ Den

Daniel prayed to God even when it was forbidden. The king’s men threw him into the lions’ den. But God sent an angel to shut the lions’ mouths, and Daniel was safe!

Lesson: Trust God no matter what—He protects His children.

⛵ Jesus Calms the Storm

One day, Jesus and His disciples were on a boat. A big storm scared everyone, but Jesus said, “Peace, be still!” and the storm stopped.

Lesson: Jesus brings peace when we are afraid.

🌈 Noah’s Ark

God told Noah to build an ark because a great flood was coming. Noah obeyed and brought animals two by two into the ark. God saved Noah and his family.

Lesson: Obeying God keeps us safe.

🌟 Abraham’s Promise

God promised Abraham that his children would be as many as the stars in the sky. Even when he was old, God kept His promise and gave him Isaac.

Lesson: God always keeps His promises.

🎶 David and Goliath

A young shepherd boy named David faced the giant Goliath. With only a sling and a stone, David defeated him because he trusted in God’s strength.

Lesson: With God, no problem is too big.

🔥 Shadrach, Meshach & Abednego

Three young men refused to worship the king’s idol. They were thrown into a fiery furnace, but God protected them, and they came out unharmed.

Lesson: Stand firm in faith, God is with you.

🧺 Jesus Feeds 5,000

One boy gave Jesus his small lunch—five loaves and two fish. Jesus blessed it and fed more than 5,000 people with food left over!

Lesson: God can do great things with small offerings.

🌟 The Birth of Jesus

In Bethlehem, Jesus was born in a stable. Angels sang, shepherds rejoiced, and wise men followed a star to worship Him.

Lesson: Jesus is God’s greatest gift to the world.

🌿 The Good Samaritan

A man was hurt on the road. Many passed by, but a Samaritan stopped to help and cared for him. Jesus said, “Go and do the same.”

Lesson: Love your neighbor by helping others.
